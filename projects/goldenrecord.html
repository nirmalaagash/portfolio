Golden Record Identification

Similar records with missing attributes from different sources are combined using HiveQL and the combined golden record is moved into PostgreSQL Database. This lets customers to recover 35% of lost records information due to errors from sources.

Technologies Used: Java, Hive, HiveQL, PostgreSQL, Azure LogicApps, Azure DataLake

Objective: To collaborate the details fetched from multiple sources and form golden record.

This was one the interesting projects that I worked on. The project was about to generate golden record. Golden Record is a record that contains complete pieces of imformation about a record.

The data is fetched from multiple sources (with missing attributes or fields of several record) and are dumped into the Azure DataLake and from the DataLake it is accessed by the HDInsights and processed in Hive to combine all the records with the help of a unique id for each record. There could be duplicates of the records and everything is handled within the HiveQL Custom Function and after combining all the fields of the record and modifying it, results in the output one unique complete record which is called a golden record. The logic of processing the data from different sources to obtaining godlen record from it is entirely written in Java. And golden records dumped in the PostgreSQL. The whole workflow is managed by Azure LogicApps which gives the seamless flow of jobs to be done.
